# Flight Simulator Tutorial

Welcome to the simple flight simulator! This tutorial will guide you through the basics of flying your aircraft.

## Getting Started

1.  Make sure the application is running. If not, follow the instructions in the `README.md` file.
2.  Open the application in your web browser.

## Flying Your Aircraft

*   **Pitch:** Use the **Up Arrow** and **Down Arrow** keys to control the pitch of the aircraft. This will make the aircraft point up or down.
*   **Roll:** Use the **Left Arrow** and **Right Arrow** keys to control the roll of the aircraft. This will make the aircraft bank left or right.

Your aircraft will automatically move forward in the direction it is pointing. Have fun exploring the skies!
